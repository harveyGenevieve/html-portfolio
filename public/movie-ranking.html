My favorite series

Top 3

180 degrees longitude passes through us

This series left a deep mark on me. I have such difficulty accepting the ending, it's so tragic. It's the total opposite of what I would have done in MC's situation and I feel that I have so much to learn from MC's emotional maturity. I am crazy enough to accept less than ideal conditions for the sake of love. I crave love to the point of not respecting myself. This series has such important life lessons for me to take away.

Addicted

This is the polar opposite of the first series; an example of how far people will go for love no matter how crazy and disadvantageous the circumstances are. An example not to follow for sure, but a story that holds much meaning and fascination to me.

I told sunset about you

Once again, heartwrenching story of impossible love. I cried so much watching this, it's great. An amazing emotional rollercoaster.
